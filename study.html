Primary School

My Schooling began at Adelaide Primary. I attended this school until i'd completed second grade and then moved up to the FNQ with my family where the remainder of my schooling was spent. However, due to Queensland's rediculous education system i was forced to repeat the second grade upon arriving in Cairns which was a huge kick in the shins. I could already read and write quite well thanks to the South Australian education system and had to re-learn it all again for a year.

Secondary School

Cairns State High School was the site of my secondary education. These were some of the best years of my life but at the same time my worst. I favoured Japanese and graduated with an OP6 putting me in the top 4% of Australian graduates.

University

This was where my life turned around and i finally found myself. At 25 i was long past my teenage years but at long last i was discovering the social aspect i missed growing up as a loner. The journey is still in play.
